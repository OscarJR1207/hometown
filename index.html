Ikogosi warm spring

One of the most popular tourist destinations in Nigeria, Ikogosi Warm Spring is a unique site where warm and cold springs merge, both maintaining their thermal properties. Located in Ekiti State, Ikogosi is situated in a dense forest and extends over a hilly landscape.
